XLI. Forever Beethoven

Original CBS Television Network
Broadcast Date: January 28,1968
Produced and Directed by Roger Englander
In this all-Beethoven program, works by the composer are performed and discussed. The assistant conductor is Paul Capolongo; the piano soloist is Joseph Kalichstein. Performances include the first movement of Symphony No. 5; Concerto in G for piano and orchestra; and the Leonore Overture No. 3.
