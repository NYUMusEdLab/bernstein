XXII. Young Performers No. 4

Original CBS Television Network
Broadcast Date: January 15, 1963
Produced and Directed by Roger Englander
Leonard Bernstein introduces three young pianists, Joan Weiner (14 years old), Claudia Hoca (12) and Pamela Paul (13), who each perform one movement of Mozart's Piano Concerto in A major. Conducting these movements, respectively, are Yuri Krasnopolsky, Zoltan Rozsnyai and Serge Fournier. Finally, 16-years-old Andre Watts plays Liszt's Piano Concerto in E-flat Major, with Bernstein conducting.
