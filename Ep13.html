XIII. Aaron Copland Birthday Party

Original CBS Television Network
Broadcast Date: February 12, 1961
Produced and Directed by Roger Englander
To celebrate Aaron Copland's 60th birthday, Bernstein discusses his music and conductors the Philharmonic in performances of An Outdoor Overture, the "Dogmatic" movement from Statements, music from the film Our Town, "Hoe-Down" from Rodeo, and baritone William Warfield singing "The Boatmen's Dance" and "I Bought Me a Cat." In conclusion, Aaron Copland conducts El Salon Mexico.
